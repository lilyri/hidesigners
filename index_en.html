About Us



Welcome to Hawaii Designers, a platform born out of passion and dedication to showcasing the extraordinary talent of Graphic Design students graduating from Kapiolani Community College's New Media Arts program (NMA).

Hawaii Designers pulsates with the energy of creativity, offering a captivating fusion of Japanese and English languages, embracing our diverse audience. Here, you will delve into a treasure trove of exceptional design projects that embody the unique perspectives and boundless imagination of our students.

With each click, you will witness the culmination of countless hours of hard work, fueled by an unwavering passion for artistic expression. From awe-inspiring visual identities to breathtaking branding campaigns, from immersive user interfaces to enchanting illustrations, every piece displayed on Hawaii Designers is a testament to the remarkable talent cultivated within KCC's NMA program.

We invite you to embark on a journey of discovery, as you navigate through the profiles of our gifted designers. Each profile is a gateway to a world of inspiration, showcasing their individual stories, impressive portfolios, and professional aspirations. Immerse yourself in their works, feel their creative pulse, and let their designs resonate with your vision.

The spirit of Hawaii Designers lies not only in celebrating the exceptional skills of our graduates but also in fostering connections between design companies and these promising individuals. We encourage you to reach out, engage, and collaborate with our talented designers. Whether you seek freelance designers, interns, or full-time professionals, Hawaii Designers is a gateway to unlocking a world of unrivaled design talent, ready to breathe life into your projects.

Let your curiosity guide you as you explore Hawaii Designers, and let the passion behind each project ignite your own creative spark. Together, we can forge new pathways in the design industry, empower the next generation of designers, and create a visual landscape that leaves a lasting impact.

We thank you for visiting Hawaii Designers and encourage you to connect with our students by reaching out to them directly for a personalized quote. Witness their passion, experience their creativity, and embark on a collaboration that will redefine the boundaries of design.

Welcome to Hawaii Designers, where talent meets opportunity, and design knows no limits.
